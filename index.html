It’s important to start your children out on the right foot ... nutrition is the key! If your child needs a drink between meals, be sure that it is water. Serve juice or milk at meal times so that teeth are not overexposed to the sugar in them. Don’t reward your children with sweets! I like to see you child so soon as their first teeth come into their mouth so we can discuss proper care and nutrition.
